## ğŸ³ Instructions
1. Whisk soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, cornstarch.
2. Sear steak in 1 Tbsp oil (high heat) approx 8 mins. Remove.
3. Cook peppers in remaining oil 4 mins.
4. Add garlic/ginger.
5. Return beef. Add sauce. Cook until glossy (2 mins).
6. Serve over rice.
